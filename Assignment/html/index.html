and digital materials,organized for reading, research, and reference.
Libraries provide access to a vast range of informationand knowledge,
they serve as spaces for study, learning, and community engagement.
Libraries can bephysical buildings or digital platforms, and they are
often managed by professionals who assist users in finding, accessing,
and using resources effectively.
